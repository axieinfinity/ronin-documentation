# Withdraw your stake
You can withdraw part of your stake back to your wallet as long as itâ€™s been three days since you last staked in. The remaining balance after withdrawal has to be at least 500,000 RON (subject to change on mainnet).

:::caution
Withdrawing a stake might lower your chance of being selected as a validator in the upcoming days, depending on how much others have staked.
:::

To withdraw your stake, follow these steps:
1. Go to **My Staking > Withdraw**.

<img src={withdrawStakeStepOne} width={480} />

2. Enter the amount manually or click **Max** to automatically fill in the maximum available amount.
3. Click **Withdraw**.

<img src={withdrawStakeStepTwo} width={416} />

4. Confirm the transaction.

<img src={confirmTransaction} width={375} />

After the transaction is confirmed, you can review its details on the **Activities** tab or check the status in [Ronin Block Explorer](https://saigon-explorer.roninchain.com/).
